# Trading Knowledge Base & Strategy Optimization

This repository serves as a centralized unit for storing knowledge, rules, and complex strategies extracted from the **Jayson Casper Trading** course and other relevant resources. It is designed to be a "deeply synchronized" workspace for optimizing trading outcomes.

## Repository Structure

- **Knowledge_Base/**: Detailed documentation of core trading concepts, organized by course modules.
  - `01_Introduction/`: Overview and foundational concepts.
  - `02_Risk_Management/`: Rules for capital preservation and position sizing.
  - `03_Market_Structure/`: Identifying trends, breaks, and shifts.
  - `04_Supply_and_Demand/`: Locating institutional zones.
  - `05_Orderflow/`: Understanding market intent through volume and price.
  - `06_Advanced_Market_Structure/`: Deep dive into complex structure.
  - `07_Liquidity/`: Identifying where the "smart money" operates.
  - `08_Ranges/`: Trading within defined boundaries.
  - `09_Wyckoff/`: Accumulation and distribution phases.
  - `10_Advanced_Range_Techniques/`: Specialized range trading strategies.
  - `11_Strategies/`: Specific, actionable trading models (e.g., Phoenix Deviation).
  - `12_All_Together/`: Synthesis of all concepts into a cohesive system.
- **Rules/**: A collection of "Black and White" rules for trade execution and management.
- **Strategies/**: Actionable trading plans and backtesting results.

## How to Use

1.  **Sync**: Use `git pull` in your local VS Code to get the latest updates from Manus.
2.  **Contribute**: Add your own notes or refine rules directly in the repository.
3.  **Optimize**: Use the documented strategies to refine your trading system.
