## The Grand Secret: The Unified Trading Plan

The "grand secret" is not a single trick or indicator. It is a **unified trading plan** that integrates all 12 pillars into a single, logical process.

### The 5-Step Trading Process:

1.  **HTF Analysis (The "Where")**:
    - **Pillar 2: Market Structure**: What is the HTF trend? (Bullish/Bearish)
    - **Pillar 8: Wyckoff**: Are we in accumulation or distribution?
    - **Pillar 7: Ranges**: Are we in a range or trending?

2.  **POI Identification (The "Why")**:
    - **Pillar 3: Supply and Demand**: Where are the HTF S&D zones?
    - **Pillar 6: Liquidity**: Where are the liquidity pools?
    - **Pillar 4: Orderflow**: Where are the unmitigated zones?

3.  **LTF Confirmation (The "When")**:
    - **Pillar 5: Advanced MS**: Wait for the LTF to align with the HTF.
    - **Pillar 10: Strategies**: Look for a specific setup (Phoenix, S2D Flip).
    - **Pillar 9: Advanced Ranges**: Use range deviations for confirmation.

4.  **Execution (The "How")**:
    - **Pillar 1: Risk Management**: Calculate your position size.
    - **Pillar 11: All Together**: Choose your entry method (High/Medium/Low risk).
    - **Pillar 4: Orderflow**: Enter on the RIMC cycle.

5.  **Trade Management (The "Optimization")**:
    - **Pillar 1: Risk Management**: De-risk and take partials.
    - **Pillar 2: Market Structure**: Trail your stop behind protected levels.
    - **Pillar 3: Supply and Demand**: Target the next opposing zone.
