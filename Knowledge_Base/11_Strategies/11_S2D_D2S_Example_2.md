## The Key Insight: "Close But Not Quite"

This lesson teaches an important nuance: **not every setup is a valid flip**.

### What Makes a Valid Flip:
1. **Clear zone** that caused a significant reaction.
2. **Clean break** with a body close.
3. **Return to the zone** (not just near it).
4. **Confirmation** on the LTF.

### What Disqualifies a Flip:
1. **Zone is unclear** or poorly defined.
2. **Break is messy** (wicks, no body close).
3. **Price doesn't return** to the exact zone.
4. **No confirmation** on the LTF.
