## The "Black and White" Rules (Integration Edition)

### The "Whole Shebang" Checklist:
1. **HTF Bias**: What is the overall trend direction?
2. **HTF Zone**: Where is the key S&D zone?
3. **HTF Flip**: Is there a valid flip setup?
4. **LTF Structure**: Does the LTF structure align?
5. **LTF Entry**: Is there a valid entry on the LTF?
6. **Risk Management**: Is the Risk/Reward favorable?

### The Key Distinction:
| Scenario | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| All 6 criteria met | Enter with confidence |
| 5 criteria met | Consider with caution |
| 4 or fewer criteria met | Skip the trade |
