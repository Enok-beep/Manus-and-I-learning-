## The "Smarter Approach" Framework

### Key Criteria for S2D/D2S Flips:
1. **Order Block with Imbalance**: Look for a zone that has an FVG (Fair Value Gap).
2. **Demand Range**: The zone should be a clear demand or supply range.
3. **Structure in Your Favor**: The overall trend should support your trade direction.

### The 50-Minute Uptrend Example:
From the transcript, Jayson describes a scenario where:
- There is a **50-minute uptrend** (internal structure).
- You see **bullish breaks** (BOS) happening.
- Structure is **breaking higher and higher**.
- This is a **perfect** setup for an S2D flip.

## The "Black and White" Rules for Theoretical Application

### Step 1: Identify the Trend
> **The trend must be clear on the LTF (e.g., 50-minute).**
> - Bullish: Higher Highs and Higher Lows.
> - Bearish: Lower Highs and Lower Lows.

### Step 2: Find the Order Block with Imbalance
> **Look for a zone that has caused a BOS and has an FVG.**
> - The FVG indicates "inefficient" price action.
> - The BOS confirms the zone's importance.

### Step 3: Wait for the Flip
> **Wait for price to break the zone and return.**
> - The break confirms the shift in sentiment.
> - The return is your entry opportunity.

### Step 4: Enter with Confluence
> **Enter when multiple factors align.**
> - S2D/D2S flip.
> - Order block.
> - FVG (imbalance).
> - Structure in your favor.

## Practical Application

### Bullish S2D Flip with Confluence:
1. **Identify a bullish trend** on the LTF (e.g., 50m).
2. **Find a Supply zone** that has been broken (BOS).
3. **Confirm the zone has an FVG** (imbalance).
4. **Wait for price to return** to the broken zone.
5. **Enter long** on LTF confirmation (CHoCH or rejection candle).
6. **Stop Loss**: Below the zone.
7. **Take Profit**: Next structural target.

### Bearish D2S Flip with Confluence:
1. **Identify a bearish trend** on the LTF.
2. **Find a Demand zone** that has been broken (BOS).
3. **Confirm the zone has an FVG** (imbalance).
4. **Wait for price to return** to the broken zone.
5. **Enter short** on LTF confirmation.
6. **Stop Loss**: Above the zone.
7. **Take Profit**: Next structural target.

> - A flip with an order block, FVG, and structure in your favor is the **best**.
