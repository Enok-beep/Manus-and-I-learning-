## The Range Reversal Model Framework

### What is a Range Reversal Model?
A **Range Reversal Model** is a strategy that identifies high-probability reversal points based on:
1. **Previous Accumulation or Distribution** evidence.
2. **Mid to High Timeframe** structure.
3. **Wyckoff-based** range analysis.

### Key Characteristics:
- **Not an everyday strategy**: This is a high-probability, low-frequency setup.
- **Requires HTF context**: Works best on mid to high timeframes.
- **Evidence-based**: Requires evidence of previous accumulation or distribution.

## The Two Range Reversal Models

### Model 1: Accumulation Reversal (Bullish)
This model is used when there is evidence of a **previous accumulation**.

**Setup Requirements:**
1. **HTF Range**: A clear consolidation on the HTF.
2. **Accumulation Evidence**: Signs of Smart Money accumulation (Wyckoff phases).
3. **Deviation Below**: Price deviates below the range low.
4. **Spring/Shakeout**: The deviation is a "Spring" (Wyckoff terminology).
5. **LTF Confirmation**: CHoCH or BOS back to the upside.

**Entry:**
- Enter long on LTF confirmation.
- Stop loss below the Spring low.
- Target: Range high, then extension.

### Model 2: Distribution Reversal (Bearish)
This model is used when there is evidence of a **previous distribution**.

**Setup Requirements:**
1. **HTF Range**: A clear consolidation on the HTF.
2. **Distribution Evidence**: Signs of Smart Money distribution (Wyckoff phases).
3. **Deviation Above**: Price deviates above the range high.
4. **UTAD/Upthrust**: The deviation is a "UTAD" (Wyckoff terminology).
5. **LTF Confirmation**: CHoCH or BOS back to the downside.

**Entry:**
- Enter short on LTF confirmation.
- Stop loss above the UTAD high.
- Target: Range low, then extension.

## How to Identify "Evidence" of Accumulation/Distribution

### Signs of Accumulation:
1. **Selling Climax (SC)**: A sharp drop with high volume.
2. **Automatic Rally (AR)**: A quick bounce after the SC.
3. **Secondary Test (ST)**: A retest of the SC low with lower volume.
4. **Multiple Tests**: Price repeatedly tests the range low.
5. **Decreasing Volume**: Volume decreases as the range develops.

### Signs of Distribution:
1. **Buying Climax (BC)**: A sharp rally with high volume.
2. **Automatic Reaction (AR)**: A quick drop after the BC.
3. **Secondary Test (ST)**: A retest of the BC high with lower volume.
4. **Multiple Tests**: Price repeatedly tests the range high.
5. **Decreasing Volume**: Volume decreases as the range develops.

## The "Black and White" Rules

### Rule 1: Identify the Range
> **The range must be clearly defined on the HTF.**
> - Mark the range high and low.
> - Draw the equilibrium (50% level).

### Rule 2: Look for Wyckoff Evidence
> **There must be evidence of accumulation or distribution.**
> - Look for the classic Wyckoff phases.
> - Confirm with volume analysis (if available).

### Rule 3: Wait for Deviation
> **The deviation must reach the -27% level or deeper.**
> - This is the "Spring" or "UTAD" zone.
> - The deviation sweeps liquidity.

### Rule 4: Confirm on LTF
> **The reversal must be confirmed on the LTF.**
> - CHoCH or BOS in the opposite direction.
> - The confirmation must occur within the deviation zone.

### Rule 5: Enter and Manage
> **Enter on LTF confirmation with stop beyond the deviation.**
> - Stop Loss: Beyond the deviation extreme.
> - Take Profit 1: Equilibrium (50%).
> - Take Profit 2: Opposite side of the range.
> - Take Profit 3: Beyond the opposite side (extension).

## The "Grand Secret" of Range Reversal Models

> **The Range Reversal Model combines Wyckoff theory with the Phoenix Deviation Technique.**
> - **Wyckoff** tells you *what* is happening (accumulation or distribution).
> - **Phoenix Deviation** tells you *when* to enter (after the deviation).
> - **Market Structure** tells you *how* to confirm (LTF BOS/CHoCH).

### Why This Works:
1. **Wyckoff evidence** increases the probability of a reversal.
2. **The deviation** traps retail traders.
3. **Smart Money uses the deviation** to fill their orders.
4. **You enter with Smart Money** and ride the reversal.
