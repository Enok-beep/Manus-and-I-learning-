# Lesson 13: Practice, Practice, Practice!

This final lesson of the Market Structure chapter is a call to action. Jayson Casper emphasizes that theory is useless without the "Muscle Memory" of seeing these patterns thousands of times.

## The Practice Framework

### 1. Backtesting
- Go back in time on your charts and identify every HH, HL, LH, LL, and BOS.
- Mark the "Protected" and "Targeted" levels for every move.
- Identify where the "Staircase" broke and where the "SFP" occurred.

### 2. Forward Testing (Paper Trading)
- Apply the rules in real-time without risking capital.
- Determine your bias every morning and see if the market follows your structural analysis.
