## The Complete Ranges Framework

### 1. Range Identification
- **Swing High**: The upper boundary of the range.
- **Swing Low**: The lower boundary of the range.
- **Equilibrium (50%)**: The midpoint that confirms the range.

### 2. Range Validation
A valid range must have:
- A clear **Swing High** and **Swing Low**.
- The **Equilibrium (50%)** must be touched/tested.
- The range must be **Significant** (aligned with HTF structure).

### 3. Premium vs. Discount
- **Premium Zone**: Above 50% (sell zone).
- **Discount Zone**: Below 50% (buy zone).
- **Equilibrium**: The 50% level (neutral zone).

### 4. Deviations
- A **Deviation** is a false breakout that grabs liquidity.
- Wait for price to **close back inside** the range.
- Enter on the **reversal** with LTF confirmation.

### 5. Finding Trades
- Look for **S&D zones** at the **Extreme Zones** (Premium or Discount).
- Wait for **LTF confirmation** (CHoCH or BOS).
- Target the **opposite side** of the range.

## The "Black and White" Rules for Ranges

| Rule | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Rule 1** | A range is confirmed when the 50% level is touched. |
| **Rule 2** | Only trade from the Extreme Zones (Premium or Discount). |
| **Rule 3** | A Deviation must close back inside the range to be valid. |
| **Rule 4** | Only trade Significant ranges (HTF aligned). |
| **Rule 5** | Always wait for LTF confirmation before entering. |

## The "Ranges Checklist"

Before trading a range, confirm:
- [ ] **Range Identification**: Swing High, Swing Low, and 50% touched.
- [ ] **Range Significance**: Aligned with HTF structure.
- [ ] **Zone Identification**: Premium or Discount zone.
- [ ] **S&D Zone**: Valid zone at the extreme.
- [ ] **LTF Confirmation**: CHoCH or BOS.
- [ ] **Risk Management**: Stop beyond the extreme, target the opposite side.
