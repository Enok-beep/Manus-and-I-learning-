## Core Concept: Deviation Zones

A **Deviation Zone** is the area beyond a range's boundary where price temporarily moves before reversing back into the range. This is the "trap" that catches retail traders.

## The Anatomy of a Deviation

### Range Structure:
1. **Range High**: The upper boundary of the consolidation.
2. **Range Low**: The lower boundary of the consolidation.
3. **Deviation High**: The area above the range high (Premium Deviation).
4. **Deviation Low**: The area below the range low (Discount Deviation).

## Deviation Zone Identification

### Using Fibonacci:
The Fibonacci tool is used to measure the **extent** of a deviation:
- **0%**: Range boundary (High or Low).
- **-27%**: First deviation level.
- **-62%**: Second deviation level.
- **-100%**: Full deviation (equal to range size).

### The "Black and White" Rule:
> **A valid deviation must:**
> 1. Break the range boundary.
> 2. Sweep liquidity (stop losses).
> 3. Show rejection (wick or reversal candle).
> 4. Return inside the range.

## Types of Deviations

### Premium Deviation (Above Range High):
- Price breaks above the range high.
- Triggers buy-side liquidity (stop losses of shorts).
- Smart Money sells into this liquidity.
- Price reverses and returns to the range.

### Discount Deviation (Below Range Low):
- Price breaks below the range low.
- Triggers sell-side liquidity (stop losses of longs).
- Smart Money buys into this liquidity.
- Price reverses and returns to the range.

## Trading Deviation Zones

### Entry Strategy:
1. **Identify the Range**: Mark the clear high and low.
2. **Wait for Deviation**: Price must break the boundary.
3. **Confirm Rejection**: Look for a reversal candle or LTF BOS.
4. **Enter on Return**: Enter as price re-enters the range.

### Stop Loss Placement:
- Beyond the deviation extreme (the wick high/low).

### Take Profit Targets:
1. **TP1**: Opposite side of the range (Equilibrium).
2. **TP2**: Opposite deviation zone.
3. **TP3**: Beyond the range (if trend continuation).

| Concept | Connection |

## The "Grand Secret" of Deviation Zones

> **Deviation Zones are the "Trap" of the market.**
> - They are designed to **stop out** retail traders.
> - They are the **entry point** for Smart Money.
> - They are the **highest probability** reversal zones.
