## Forex vs. Crypto Differences

| Aspect | Forex | Crypto (BTC) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Volatility** | Lower, smoother moves | Higher, more erratic |
| **Session Times** | Defined (London, NY, Asia) | 24/7 |
| **Liquidity** | Very high | Variable |
| **Structure Clarity** | Often cleaner | Can be noisier |

### Practical Implication:
- Forex is often **easier to read** for beginners because the structure is cleaner.
- Crypto requires more **patience** and **discipline** due to higher volatility.

## The Entry Process (Forex)

### Step-by-Step Process:
1. **6H/4H Analysis**: Identify the swing bias and the key swing highs/lows.
2. **1H Analysis**: Wait for internal structure to align with the swing bias.
3. **15m/5m Confirmation**: Look for a CHoCH or BOS in your direction.
4. **Execute**: Enter the trade with your stop behind the relevant structural point.
